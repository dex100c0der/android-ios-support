GPS JOYSTICK SETTINGS

Various settings for GPS Joystick mocking app to spoof.

PRE-MARCH 2017 ASP

PHONE:

  1. Open Settings on your Android phone.

    2. click image to enlarge

  2. Scroll down and tap Developer options.

    3. click image to enlarge

  3. Look for and tap Mock Location app, select your cloned gps joystick app.

    4. click image to enlarges

  4. Turn on and set your GPS/ Location to High Accuracy.

    5. click image to enlarge

  5. Reboot your phone if needed.

GPS JOYSTICK APP:

  1. Open gps joystick app then tap Menu (upper left).

    2. click image to enlarge

  2. Tap Settings.

    3. click image to enlarge

  3. Scroll down and enable "ENABLE INDIRECT MOCKING".

    4. click image to enlarge
NOTE: You can enable Prevent screenshots/ Display Distance Info if you like. It doesn't matter.
