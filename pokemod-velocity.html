Pokemod Velocity

Pokemod Team
Forwards GPS coordinates to GPS mocking app for rooted/ non-rooted devices. Pokemod Velocity IS NOT A SPOOFING APP.

SUPPORTED GPS MOCKING APP:

GPS JoyStick (appninjas)

INSTALL/ SET-UP:

  1. Go to Playstore and search for "Pokemod Velocity".

    2. click image to enlarge

  2. Download and install the app.

    3. click image to enlarge

  3. Launch the App and toggle "Enable Service".

    4. click image to enlarge

  4. Click "Set Auto Lunch" and select Pokemon GO app in the list then close.

    5. click image to enlarge


Set-up with Discord app:

  1. Open Discord app, go to Pokedex100 server then look for "100community" channel.

    2. click image to enlarge

  2. Click the provided coord link. "Click for Coords" (for free members) and "Donor" (for Donors).

    3. click image to enlarge

  3. Either click ""Trust this domain"" or "Comfirm" if prompted.

    4. click image to enlarge

  4. Log-in again for one time discord authoriztion.

    5. click image to enlarge

  5. Click "Authorize".

    6. click image to enlarge

  6. When you are prompted to choose what app to use to open the link, choose "Velocity" and make sure to selet "Always".

    7. click image to enlarge

    • Once the initial set-up is done, all you need to do next time is to click a coord link and will be teleported immediately to that location.

TIP:

Use Split Screen if your phone support it, or just switch between discord app (to get coords) and pogo app.
